Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can sign up using email, Google Sign-In, or Apple Sign-In. Open the app and tap "Sign Up" to get started.

How do I enable push notifications?

Go to Settings → Notifications and enable notifications for Scores Hub. You can also manage notification preferences within the app.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently delete your account and all associated data.

I'm seeing incorrect match times

Make sure your device timezone is set correctly. The app uses your device's timezone to display match times.

How do I report a bug?

Please email us at scoreshub@tryscoreshub.com with details about the issue, including your device model and iOS version.
